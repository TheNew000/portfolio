About

While I am still in my early stages of full stack development, I am skilled in the programs and frameworks I have familiarized myself with and am a hungry and eager learner.

I am an alumnus of DigitalCrafts an intensive and immersive 16 Week program that focuses on both the MEAN and LAMP stacks. I'm one of the rare NYC natives that were actually born on the island, so my ability to acclimate to new places and cultures leaves me open to opportunities around the world!

I absolutely love data: from collection to organizing and of course, analyzing. I want to build modern complex and elaborate algorithms that not only track the trends that we're looking for but are dynamic enough to learn from the data and help enlighten us to correlations that haven't been understood or noticed yet. I am eager to join other motivated developers and companies with the same goals and passions that I feel about learning and improving our world. Please check out my work and feel free to reach out to me!
